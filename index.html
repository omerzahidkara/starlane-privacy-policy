Privacy Policy for Starlane

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Voidpath ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile game Starlane ("the Game").

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the Game.

1. Information We Collect

Starlane does not require users to create an account or log in.

However, the Game may collect certain information automatically through third-party services used in the app, including advertising and analytics-related services.

This information may include:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App interactions and usage information
  • Diagnostics and crash data
  • Performance data

2. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Operate and improve the Game
  • Maintain app stability and performance
  • Show ads, including rewarded and banner ads
  • Measure ad performance and user engagement
  • Detect technical issues and prevent abuse

3. Advertising

Starlane uses third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob, to display ads.

These services may collect and process data such as device identifiers, IP address, and app interaction data in order to provide, personalize, measure, and improve advertisements.

You can review Google’s privacy information here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

However, data may be shared with third-party service providers that help operate the Game, such as:

  • Advertising providers
  • Analytics and diagnostics providers
  • Platform and infrastructure providers

These third parties process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

5. Children's Privacy

Starlane is not directed specifically to children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information in a way that should not have occurred, please contact us and we will review the situation.

6. Data Retention

We do not directly maintain user account databases for Starlane.

Certain data collected automatically by third-party services may be retained for as long as necessary to provide their services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to work with trusted service providers, but no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including the right to request access, deletion, or restriction of certain data processing.

Because some data may be handled by third-party providers, requests may also need to be directed to those providers where applicable.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
